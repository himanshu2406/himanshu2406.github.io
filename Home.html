About Us

Founder President of the Organisation Mr. Sushil Gaikwad Sir is working for HIV positive people from last 10 years 2013 to 2022. Actually there was no platform for HIV positive people to interact and marry . He found that due to lack of platform, many HIV infected persons married healthy persons and destroyed the happiness of both the families, furthermore many boys and girls even committed suicide due to this where the lives of many were ruined. Sushil Gaikwad sir visited people from home to home knowing their happiness, sorrows & worries. He inspired them to live their life with a new prospect.

Success

With the help of this website, 39 marriages have been successfully conducted by us since the time of lockdown till now. And many have given birth to healthy children. There is no fraud here, all the information (profile) is cross checked here in the beginning and the entire team working here is HIV (positive) itself so the work is done with certainty and care. 

Confidential

Here all the information is kept confidential and this organization only works for HIV positive people.
